Course Overview

Duration:4 weeks (16 lessons)
Time per lesson:20-30 minutes per lesson
Practice required:2-3 hours per week on court

Goal: Play your first confident match with proper technique and basic strategy

What You'll Achieve

  • Master the fundamental strokes and serves
  • Understand all basic rules and court etiquette
  • Play effectively using the walls as your ally
  • Complete rallies of 10+ shots consistently
  • Feel confident joining any beginner group or match

Equipment Needed

Padel racket (beginner-friendly recommended)
Padel balls (not tennis balls!)
Court shoes with good lateral support
Comfortable athletic clothing
